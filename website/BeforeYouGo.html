Know before you go

What to bring

Payments Accepted:

We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express at most of our locations. Some food and retail carts may not have credit card capabilities, but there are several ATMs throughout the the park for your convenience. We do not accept personal checks.

We don't sell gift cards but we do sell Creature Cash which can be used like cash at any location inside the Zoo and Zoombezi Bay. Creature Cash can be purchased at Guest Relations.

Food and Drink

Outside food beverages (excluding alcohol) are permitted, but no straws to protect our animals

Service Animals

Service animals are welcome but pets are not permitted in the Zoo. We do not offer a kennel service and we do not recommend leaving animals in your car while you visit.

What NOT to bring

No weapons

No laser pointers

Items that are NOT permitted

The following items are not permitted: Firearms, knives, glass containers, balloons, bicycles, skateboards and other skating equipment (including hover boards), large beach umbrellas, lounge chairs, radios, rafts and floatation devices, grills or open fires.

Getting Here

Parking

Parking at the zoo is $10 per vehicle for those without a membership

COTA Bus Service

Take COTA's Zoo Bus on weekends and holidays May 11 - Sept. 2, 2019 and daily from May 28 - Aug. 30, 2019. (Be sure to check back in the spring for the 2020 schedule!)

COTA Zoo Bus schedule:
COTA's Zoo Bus leaves from the Statehouse & High at 8 AM, 9:30 AM, 2:30 PM, 6 PM and 7:30 PM making stops at High & Broad, High & Long, Spring & Wall, Spring & Marconi, Griggs Dam Park & Ride and Dublin Park & Ride before it gets to the Zoo.

COTA's Zoo Bus leaves from the Zoo at 8:45 AM, 10:15 AM, 3:15 PM, 6:45 PM and 8:15 PM making stops at Dublin Park & Ride, Griggs Dam Park & Ride, Long & High, Third & Gay, and Third & Broad before it arrives at the Statehouse on State near High.

COTA Bus Fare:
$2 each way
*COTA discount pass holders (Seniors and Key ID Cards) and children 48” tall and over through 12 years of age is just $1. Children under 48” tall are FREE. Limit three children with adult. Does not include groups.

COTA Zoo Bus Rider Discount:
Riders will receive a transfer/coupon from their driver to receive discounted Zoo admission for $3/person or Zoombezi Bay admission for $10 OFF/person. (The discount is not valid with other offers.)

COTA's Zoo Bus service is supported by the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium. Please click here for the full 2019 Zoo Bus schedule. (The 2020 Zoo Bus schedule will be available in the spring.)

Getting around the Zoo

Please note we do not offer transportation once you are inside the Zoo, so we recommend you wear comfortable shoes. Wheelchairs, electric scooters and strollers are available to rent, while supplies last.

You may exit and re-enter the park in the same day, just be sure to receive a hand-stamp on your way out. If you do not receive a hand-stamp you will not be permitted back into the park.

We offer nursing stations and family restrooms throughout the park. To view the park map click here.

Wagons and strollers are permitted at the Zoo but please obey stroller parking signs at designated locations.
