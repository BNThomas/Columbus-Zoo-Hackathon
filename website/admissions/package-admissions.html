Our military discount policy grants a $2 discount on up to 6 Columbus Zoo and Aquarium tickets with a valid military ID.

Bulk Discount

If you are part of a group of 15 or more people, then you may enjoy a $5 discount to the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium.

Senior Discount

Seniors can enjoy half off admission to the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium every Tuesday with a valid form of ID (driver's license or state ID).

Local Discount

Residents of Franklin County can enjoy half off admission to the Columbus Zoo and Aquarium every Monday-Wednesday with a valid form of ID (driver's license, state ID, or utility bill).
